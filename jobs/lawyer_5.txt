Job Title: Labor Law Attorney
Location: Miami, Florida (on-site)
Job Type: Full-time

Responsibilities:

Represent employees and employers in labor and contractual disputes.
Provide legal advice on workers' rights, labor policies, and employment law compliance.
Manage cases involving discrimination, wrongful termination, and violations of labor rights.
Review and draft employment contracts and severance agreements.
Requirements:

Law degree with at least 3 years of experience in labor law.
Knowledge of current Florida labor laws and federal regulations.
Ability to work effectively with unions and employee committees.
Desired Skills:

Strong litigation and conflict resolution skills.
Experience in negotiating and mediating labor disputes.
